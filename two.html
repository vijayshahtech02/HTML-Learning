Nashik Blog

Nashik, the "Wine Capital of India" and a revered pilgrimage site, offers a perfect blend of spirituality, history, and vineyards on the banks of the Godavari River. Known for Sula Vineyards, Trimbakeshwar Temple, and Pandavleni Caves, this Maharashtra city is ideal for a 2-3 day weekend getaway from Mumbai or Pune


Top Attractions & Experiences

Vineyards: Visit Sula Vineyards York Winery, or Soma Vineyards for wine tasting and scenic views of Gangapur Dam.

Spirituality: Explore the holy site of PanchavatiRamkund, and the famous Trimbakeshwar Temple (one of the 12 Jyotirlingas).

History & Adventure: Hike to the 1st-century BC Pandavleni Caves, the historic Anjaneri Fort, or the steep Harihar Fort.

Nature & Landscapes: Experience the lush, green landscapes during the monsoon season, particularly at Dudhsagar Falls
